ABOUT UNIVERSAL CHILD CARE FOUNDATION


Universal Child Care Foundation is a non-governmental organisation that was set up in August 2015. Universal Childcare foundation seeks to support less-privileged children who without such support would not be able to cope with the standard of living in Ghana and also develop themselves in the environment in which they find themselves.

Universal Childcare Foundation works closely with children by taking care of their day-to-day needs. With our collective support, we can all come together and change the living conditions of needy and homeless children by making their lives meaningful.

Universal Childcare Foundation provides a good and safe atmosphere for the children which enables and helps the developing child to achieve his or her full potential. We conduct various donation programs through which we try to provide support to children in deprived communities who without such support would not be able to cope with the harsh conditions of living in Ghana. Universal Childcare Foundation also organises educational and motivational programs which helps the children in terms of social awareness and their educational well-being.
